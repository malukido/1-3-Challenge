The Rules
🎰
Game
$1/3 No-Limit Hold'em Only
The challenge is played exclusively at $1/3 NLHE. No other stakes, no other formats — just the one table where it all counts.
🪙
Buy-In
100 Big Blinds — $300
Every session starts with a standard 100 big blind buy-in of $300. Consistent buy-ins keep the tracking clean and the challenge honest.
🛑
Stop Loss
$600 Per Session
A hard stop loss of $600 per session protects the bankroll. If losses hit $600, the session ends — no exceptions, no chasing.
Meet Lindsay
🃏 Pro since 2014

Born in Hawaiʻi.
Built in Vegas.

Lindsay grew up in Hawaiʻi before making the bold move to Las Vegas at 22 — to chase her lifelong dream of playing poker. For the past 12 years, she has been a full-time professional poker player.

Her regular limit is $2/5 No-Limit Hold'em, but she has tested herself at stakes as high as $100/200/200 NLH. Over the years, she has split her time between the cash game grind and the tournament circuit. She has also had the privilege of traveling the world and playing in different countries and cities.

Right now, she is locked in on cash games exclusively — and the $1/3 Challenge is her latest mission. The goal is $10,000 in profit from the ground up. Next up? She will be doing more challenges at $2/5, $5/10, and $10/20.
